Lake Powell

Lake powell is a large lake, is spans acorss southern utah, into Arizona. It has sadly lost about 100ft of depth, it is still a huge deep body of water, perfect for fishing, tubing, and other water based recreational activited. The deepest point of lake powell is over 400 feet, before the large drought, and the selling of lake water, its depth was once over 500 ft. Depsite the water loss, it is still a hot spot for Utah.
